What iService is

iService is a trust standard for commerce. We define a measurable covenant for ethical service delivery, verify compliance through audit, and publish a registry of certified businesses.

iService is not a marketplace. It is not a review platform. It is not a consultancy.

Brand Identity

Core Values

ICE-S governs every certification decision.

I

Integrity

Truth over convenience. Evidence over performance.

C

Customer Stewardship

Dignity in every interaction. Clear communication and fair treatment.

E

Excellence

Measured. Documented. Enforced. Continuous improvement is required.

S

Selflessness

Public trust above private gain. The seal is protected, not sold.
